About

Here's a lil about me:

Hi I'm Gabe! I'm currently a third year student at UC Berkeley pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science. One of the things I love the most more than anything else is creating meaningful technology and that’s exactly what I strive to achieve through whatever I do. Whether it's an internships, a personal projects, or an extracurricular, I always try to find purpose in whatever I do. Serving those in the communities around me, usually through STEM related outreach events, is also something I find joy in. Around campus, I’m currently involved in organizations like Quant at Berkeley and The Berkeley Project.

When I’m not diving into a project or reading up on the latest advancements in technology, you’ll probably find me playing basketball (Go Lakers!) or exploring new places with my friends. I also love dancing and am part of AFX, a dance organization at Berkeley. Cooking and eating good food are also great stress relievers for me. Feel free to check out the rest of this website as well as my resume to learn more about me! If you have any questions, don’t hesitate to reach out!
